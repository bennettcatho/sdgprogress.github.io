About this project

This project is part of a final thesis for the Master's program in Data & Artificial Intelligence at the Catholic University of Lille. The primary objective is to examine the advancements of various countries across the 17 SDGs outlined in the 2030 Agenda. The objective is to identify which countries have made the most significant progress compared to others, both for each individual SDG and overall, in order to understand the actions they have implemented. This knowledge can then be leveraged to support other countries in achieving their SDGs more effectively.

ABOUT

This project is part of a final thesis for the Master's program in Data & Artificial Intelligence at the Catholic University of Lille. The first objective is to examine the advancements of various countries across the 17 SDGs outlined in the 2030 Agenda. The second objective is to predict what the score will be in 2030 for each country and each SDG. The third objective is to predict in which year the country will achieve the goal. The fourth objective is to identify the clusters of countries that have similar progress in the SDGs.
