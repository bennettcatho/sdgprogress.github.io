Progress Rank

Select the years you want to compare. If you choose multiple years in "Period 1" their average will be calculated. The same applies to "Period 2" A year selected in the first dropdown cannot be chosen again in the second. You can also select countries and goals to compare. The table will display each country's score for each goal in the selected period. If you select multiple goals, their average will be calculated based on available data. If a country has no data for a specific goal in a given year, the score will be 0. However, if data exists for some goals but not others, only the available scores will be shown. You can sort the table by clicking on the arrows next to the column names.

ABOUT

This project is part of a final thesis for the Master's program in Data & Artificial Intelligence at the Catholic University of Lille. The first objective is to examine the advancements of various countries across the 17 SDGs outlined in the 2030 Agenda. The second objective is to predict what the score will be in 2030 for each country and each SDG. The third objective is to predict in which year the country will achieve the goal. The fourth objective is to identify the clusters of countries that have similar progress in the SDGs.
